The complete manuscript had 5 quires of 12 (quires 1-2, 4-5, 7; 60 folios); one of 14 (the third) and one of 10 (the sixth): 84 folios in total. There are no missing folios. The recto of the the last folio of quire 6 (folio 72) is blank, as is the last folio of the last quire (folio 84, following MA). SQ TT MO and CY are severely abbreviated. L28 finishes at the top of the first column folio 72r with a rubric signalling that TM should follow, and the rest of the sheet is blank, as is 72v.
