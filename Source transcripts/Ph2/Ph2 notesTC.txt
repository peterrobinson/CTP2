==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Geneva (Coligny), Foundation Martin Bodmer Cod. Bodmer 48. Mosser: Ph2.

==== Original construction  ====

30 quires of 8 (240 folios) and two quires of six (quires 27 and 29): 252 folios in total, or 248 if the last quire was a four. 

==== Missing folios  ====

The first folio is missing from the first quire (folios 1), without any loss of text; the second folio is missing from quire 30 (folio 230); and the last four folios of the last quire (presuming this was a regular quire of eight) are missing. 


 ==== Added folios  ====
 
None.

==== Absent text corresponding to the missing folio  ====
 
PA 409-436 (folio 230); PA 988-end (250ff).

==== Notes  ====

The traditional numbering skips 229, corresponding to the missing folio 230.

Note omission of PA 342-355 -- near pornographic section dealing with clothes! and 358-362 on the same page 228r; 596-600 omitted on 236v
