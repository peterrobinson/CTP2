==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Tokyo, Takamiya Collection, MS 24. Mosser: Ds1.

==== Original construction  ====

35 quires of eight folios, and a final quire of two folios: 282 folios.  

==== Missing folios  ====

None.

==== Added folios ====
 
None.

====  Notes  ====

The Canterbury Tales occupies folios 1r through 274r, and is followed by Lydgate's Life of St Margaret, on folios 275r to the end of the manuscript.
