Ds1 notes
This manuscript is comprised of 35 quires of eight folios, and a final quire of two folios, hence 282 folios.  There is no loss of leaves.  The Canterbury Tales occupies folios 1r through 274r, and is followed by Lydgate's Life of St Margaret, on folios 275r to the end of the manuscript.
