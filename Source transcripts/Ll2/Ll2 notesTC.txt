==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Wiltshire, Longleat House (Marquess of Bath) Longleat MS 29. Mosser: Ll2.

==== Notes  ====

A complex manuscript created by combination of four booklets, according to Mosser. PA is in booklet 2, fols. 81r-128v in Mosser's numbering, along with several other religious texts. We do not attempt any renumbering of the folios.
