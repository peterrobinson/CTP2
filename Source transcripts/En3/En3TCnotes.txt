==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

London, British Library, Egerton MS 2864. Mosser: En3.

==== Original construction  ====

Irregular. 26 quires containing mostly half-sheet singletons sewn together into quires containing from 2 to 18 folios: 341 folios in total.  

==== Missing folios  ====

None.

==== Added folios ====

None.

====  Notes  ====

DWM suggests the last folio, currently holding two leaves, was originally a quire of 4; he numbers the second last folio as '240', correctly '340'.
Folios 98 and 99 are bound in reverse order.
