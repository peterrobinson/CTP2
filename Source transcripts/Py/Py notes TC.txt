==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

London, Royal College of Physicians MS 388. Mosser: Py.

==== Original construction  ====

18 quires of 20 (360 folios) and one of 16 (the twelfth): 376 folios.

==== Missing folios  ====

The two outer bifolia (1.20, 2.19) and the third leaf (folios 257-259, 275-276) are missing from quire 14, as are the last seven leaves of the last quire (folios 370-376).

==== Absent text corresponding to the missing folio  ====
 
PR 92-end, L25 1-13 (folios 257-259); TM 271-334 (folios 275-276); PA 989-end (370ff).

==== Notes  ====

Note the eyeskup of KT 1860-61, around the page break on the word "disconfortynge" -- 1859 and 1861. L20 is copied twice: once on 110v, and again on 245v (crossed out, and then followed by L15, finishing at top of 246v with the rest of the sheet blank).  This is followed by SH -- perhaps on the premise that the first line of SH mentions a merchant.
