The complete manuscript had 37 quires of 8 (296 folios) and one quire of four (quire 32), for a total of 300 folios.  The outer bifolium 1.8 is missing from the first quire (folios 1 and 8); the third leaf is missing from quire 28 (folio 219); and the outer bifolium 1.8 is missing from the last quire (folios 293 and 300). There is a minor error in Mosser's foliation: he numerates quires 33 to 37, five quires of 8 and hence forty folios, as "250-290": it should be "250-289", with the six folios of the last quire (38) foliated as "290-295".

Absent text corresponding to the missing folios is as follows:  GP 1-62 (folio 1); GP 414-471 (folio 8);  likely no missing text on 219? NU finishes on half way down 218v; PH *may* at top of 220r -- check when image is available. What was between them? image for 218v/220r missing, so can't check whether something follows NU on 218v, and whether PH starts on 220r
NP 556-620 (folio 293)

The text is so faded from L28 on that no attempt was made to identify the text on each page: so no files created for L28 TM.
