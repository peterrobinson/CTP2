==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Chetham’s Library, Manchester, MS Mun. A.4.104. Mosser: CT.

==== Notes  ====

As this manuscript contains only two items from the Tales, NU and PR, nineteen folios only from a manuscript of some 287 folios, we do not attempt a refoliaition. 179r is blank, with PR finishing at the base of 178v.
