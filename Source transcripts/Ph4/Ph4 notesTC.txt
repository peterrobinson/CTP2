==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

San Marino, Huntington Library HM 140. Mosser: Ph4.

==== Original construction  ====

The Clerk's Tale falls in the fifth and sixth quires of this complex and varied manuscript. The four preceding quires are a 14 (q. 1) and three 16s (2-4), a total of 62 folios. CL begins on the 6th leaf of the fifth quire, folio 68. 

==== Missing folios  ====

The eighth leaf is missing from the fifth quire (folio 70). 

==== Absent text corresponding to the missing folio  ====
 
CL 189-260 (folio 70).

==== Notes  ====

CL finishes on the sixth leaf of the sixth quire, folio 84.
