London, British Library Additional MS 5140 (Mosser)

For the principles of this description, see the refoliation article.

Images

The images on this site were taken from a microfilm copy created by the British Library. We are grateful to the British Library for their support of this project over many years.

Original construction

36 quires of 12: 432 folios.

Missing folios

Leaves 3 and 4 in quire 2 (folios 15 and 16); leaf 8 in quire 25 (folio 296); leaf 10 in in quire 26 (folio 310); the last four leaves of the last quire (folios 429-432).

Added folios

None.

Absent text corresponding to the missing folios

KT 111-214 (folios 15 and 16).  There is no missing text corresponding with folio 296 (295v finishs with the incipit of L33  and 297r starts with the first line of L33); with folio 310 (309v finishes with CY 676 and 311r starts with CY 677); with folios 429-432.

Notes

The most recent British Library foliation (followed by Mosser) includes an additional leaf at the beginning of the manuscript, excluded in this foliation. Our foliation excludes the final lost four leaves.
