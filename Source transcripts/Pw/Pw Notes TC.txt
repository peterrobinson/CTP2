==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Sussex, Petworth House National Trust MS 7. Mosser: Pw.

==== Original construction  ====

38 regular quires of eight (304 folios) with a final quire of 4: 308 folios.

==== Missing folios  ====

The last folio of the last quire (308). The text of the Tales finishes on the third folio of the last quire, so there is no loss of text.

==== Absent text corresponding to the missing folio  ====
 
None.
