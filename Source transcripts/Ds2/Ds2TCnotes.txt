==== Manuscript name and Mosser link  ====

Chatsworth House, Derbyshire (Home of the Duke of Devonshire), “Devonshire Fragment. Mosser: Ds2.

====  Notes  ====

Two single folios, numbered 1 and 2, containing two segments of ML: 752-826 on folio 1, 978-1046 on folio 2. The text between the two segments would have occupied two folios.
